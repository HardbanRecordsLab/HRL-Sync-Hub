## ❓ FAQ

**Q: How much does HRL take?**
A: Standard: 8% | Creator Tier: 5% | Professional: 3%

**Q: Can I set my own prices?**
A: Yes! We provide suggested prices, but you control everything.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Payouts within 24-48 hours after license purchase.

**Q: What if someone disputes a license?**
A: We have a dispute resolution process. You keep the money 
   unless proven invalid. Our team will help.

**Q: Do I need ISRC/ISWC codes?**
A: No, but it's recommended. We can generate ISRCs for you.

**Q: Can I use my DAW plugin?**
A: Yes! ProTools, Logic, Ableton, FL Studio, Cubase supported.

**Q: How do I get more licenses?**
A: Better metadata, competitive pricing, active promotion,
   quality music. We'll suggest optimizations.

**Q: Can I remove a track?**
A: Yes, but active licenses remain valid. You can hide it
   from marketplace but must honor existing deals.

**Q: Is there a minimum payout?**
A: No, you get paid for every license, even if it's $10.

**Q: Can I integrate with YouTube/Spotify?**
A: YouTube Content ID: Yes. Spotify etc.: You distribute
   separately (via DistroKid, etc.), but manage both in HRL.
