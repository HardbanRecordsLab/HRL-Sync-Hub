## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I use a license in multiple projects?**
A: Depends on license type. Most licenses are per-project.
   Some are multi-use. Check the license details.

**Q: What happens if I don't credit the artist?**
A: You're technically in breach. Artists can file dispute.
   Recommendation: Always credit. It's easy and right.

**Q: Can I edit the music?**
A: Usually no. License grants rights to use as-is.
   If you need edits, contact artist for remix.

**Q: What if my project makes money?**
A: One-time license fee = all your revenue is yours.
   No ongoing royalties to artist.

**Q: Can I resell the music?**
A: No. License is for your use only.
   Cannot sublicense or resell.

**Q: What if music gets Copyright Claim on YouTube?**
A: Normal! License handles this. You keep revenue.
   Artist gets PRO royalties separately.

**Q: Do I need insurance?**
A: For major projects, yes. License agreement provided
   gives you legal protection for licensed use.

**Q: Can I use for client work (as an agency)?**
A: Yes, if license allows "commercial" or specific use.
   Client work may require commercial license.

**Q: What if artist removes track?**
A: Your license remains valid. You keep your copy forever.
   Artist can remove from marketplace, not from your account.

**Q: How do I get refunds?**
A: 14-day refund policy if unused.
   Contact support within 14 days.
